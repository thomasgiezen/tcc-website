Brand / Visual Identity
The Client

Apropet is a theater company comprised by three young actresses. They have begun their journey wanting to bring the magic of theater to the people. But in this case is not people coming to the play, is the play coming to the people's house.

The Challenge

The three young actresses and their essence shape what Apropet is at its core. Our mission was to help them understand their key values and to translate this values into a powerful visual identity they feel related to.

Asking the right questions allowed us to understand the project and engage with the people involved.
As a starting point, we define the concepts Apropet felt related to.
