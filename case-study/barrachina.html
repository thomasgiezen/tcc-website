The Client

Barrachina is a small company helping their clients in the documentation and handling of administrative, tax-related and legal paperwork. With a recent major change in the management of the company, Barrachina wants to refresh their values and identity after fifteen years of activity.

The Challenge

interludio helped Barrachina to look deep into who they were and find out what goals did they have as a company. This led us to a thorough work of redefining a fifteen-years-old company. Once the new Barrachina was defined, we were able to create a visual identity and a website that reflected the new values.

We worked closely with Barrachina to understand & define their core values.
